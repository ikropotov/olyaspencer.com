Greta Van Kool
Mezzo Soprano

Welcome to the official page of Greta Van Kool, a dynamic mezzo soprano with a rich, expressive voice and a love for telling stories through music. Greta's performances span centuries of operatic and vocal literature, from the elegant baroque of Handel, to the romantic expressiveness of Vaccai, and the dramatic flair of Britten.

Repertoire Highlights

  • Handel – Ariodante, Rinaldo, Serse
  • Vaccai – Giulietta e Romeo, vocal method
  • Britten – The Rape of Lucretia, A Midsummer Night’s Dream
